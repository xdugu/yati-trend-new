Shipping

We ship our products with the help of GLS (internationally as well) and Foxpost.

If spent 25.000 Ft, shipping is FREE in the whole area of Hungary.

Shipping cost can be calculated by clicking on the basket icon before checking out.
Countries we ship to: Hungary, Austria, Germany, United Kingdom. In case of other countries, please contact us and we will try to find a way for you.

Order processing and delivery

The process of the orders is done on working days from 8-19 o’clock. If order is made out of this time range, then the order will only be processed the next working day. Accepted and confirmed orders are shipped between 2-5 working days. If the item is out of stock, it may take 1-2 weeks to purchase.
Our company is not responsible for any technical changes to the ordered product due to prior notice to the supplier or for reasons beyond its control.

Packages are delivered on working days only. For shipping address it is advisable to give an address where you can be available at any time, it can be your workplace as well.
In case of non-taken, returned packages, the shipping and return charges will be charged to the customer, and can only be resent once the payment of the package is transferred to our bank account.
If you have any questions about the operation of our store, orders or delivery, you can contact us either on email or telephone. Please find these information in the “Contact us”.
In case of loss of the parcel or notice of damage on the parcel at the time of delivery shall be indicated immediately on the delivery document. Failure to do so will result in loss of rights. In the absence of the delivery document, any other document related to the item and shipping must be immediately reported to the post office within a time limit of 3 days from the date of delivery.

GLS

You can track your parcel here with the given tracking number you get. Delivery time is 1-3 working days.
  • Home delivery
    Consignments are delivered between 8 am and 5 pm on working days. Our partner sends a notification to the recipient via SMS or email about the arrival of the shipment and details of the receipt.
    If the courier does not find anybody at the delivery address, your parcel may be taken to a local collection point or post office so that you can collect it at a time of your convenience.
    A courier attempts to deliver the parcel three times. In all instances you will be advised of the delivery attempt by email, or a card will be left by the courier, with instructions on where to collect your parcel, or options on rearranging the delivery for another time.

Foxpost

Important to note: At the terminals you can only pay by credit/debit card.
  • Delivery to automated parcel terminal
    After giving the parcel to FOXPOST, they deliver it to the terminals in 1 working day.When your parcel has arrived to the automated parcel terminal, we will send you a notification via text and e-mail. Use this unique code – known only to you – to open the appropriate locker of the Terminal.
    Important: keep hold of the text message; you might need it later if you want to return the package. If you choose pay on delivery, use your credit/debit card to pay at the Terminal, just like you would do it at any shop.
    Your parcel is stored in the terminal for maximum 3 days. Find a parcel terminal

    Terminal delivery fee: 700 Ft ~ 2, 2 euro
    Pay on delivery: +125 Ft ~ 0,4 euro
