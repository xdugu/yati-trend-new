Prices and payments

  1. Payment and currency

    Prices shown on the website are in Hungarian forint (HUF), Euro (EUR) and English pound.
    There are more ways for paying: on a safety way through PayPal, bank transfer or with pay on delivery (this one is only available in the area of Hungary). In this case, the amount of the order will be paid by the customer upon receipt of the products and will include an additional cost.
    As long as you choose the option of paying by bank transfer, we will send you the information needed when transferring by email.

  2. Additional tax fees

    The prices displayed for the products are exempt from VAT as our business is declared outside the tax area.
    Prices do not include delivery fees. They are added at check-out, depending on which delivery method you choose.

  3. Delivery fees

    • Spending above 25.000 Ft, shipping is FREE (in the area of Hungary)
    • For further information, please click on “Shipping”
    • In case of pay on delivery the pay on delivery extra cost will be added to the shipping cost.

  4. Other information

    If the operator shows a defective price next to the product, despite the diligence expected from him/her, and the price of the product differs from its generally accepted price, the operator is not obliged to provide the product at the wrong price, but is obliged to offer the buyer the real price and the possibility of buying it. If the buyer does not wish to make use of this option, he has the right to unilaterally withdraw from the contract.
    During shipping no additional packaging costs will be charged. Detailed shipping rates are included in the terms of this contract, under Shipping.
