Use of Cookies

We use cookies to understand the different number of users that visit our site. When a new user visits, they are assigned a random unique id When you accept the use of cookies of our site, the data that is collected is listed below:

  • Which pages of our site you visited
  • How often you visited our website
  • Which country you visited from
  • Which device was used to visit our site (Mobile phone/ desktop computer)

We don't track what you do on other websites and we don't sell this data to third parties. This data is purely for us to understand the amount of traffic on our site so we can give you the best experience posible. We also use this data to know which of our products are the most viewed so we can adjust stocking in the future. We don't link cookies with your personal data so we will never be able to personally identify you.
