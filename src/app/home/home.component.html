About Us

Yati-Trend clothes are designed to merge African and European Fashion.

My husband, Yayra is from Ghana where wearing colourful and patterned garments are very fashionable. Our designs are made to fuse the african flare and color into european clothing.

The aim with the clothes we design is to encourage people to be more open to wearing coloured and patterned clothes which shows uniqueness and confidence . . .
